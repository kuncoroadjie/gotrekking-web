About Us

GoTrekking is an Indonesian mountain trekking service provider. Initially limited to a group of climbers. But over time we want to invite and make it easier for mountaineers or ordinary people who want to unwind by climbing mountains.


Then in 2013 we agreed to transform into an open trip service provider with a concentration on trekking to the mountains. We are experienced and reliable. More than 600 people have used this service.
